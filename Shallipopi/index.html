About Shallipopi

Pluto President

Crown Uzama known professionally as Shallipopi, is a Nigerian rapper, singer, and songwriter. He rose to mainstream fame in 2023 following the release of his single "Elon Musk", which peaked at number one on the Nigeria Top 100 Apple Music.

Shallipopi began his Music career at an early age and his passion for music made him venture into creating music. Although, shallipopi started by posting his freestyles on TikTok and other social media platforms.
